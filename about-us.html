About Us

At Confi-Dental Clinic & Implant Centre, our philosophy is to treat our patients, as we would like to be treated. Right from the moment you step through our doors you will be invited into a warm and friendly atmosphere. We pride ourselves on providing great dental care, without losing the touch for a Family owned practice.

By investing in the latest in Dental Technology and State of the art modern facilities, coupled with impeccable sterilization protocols, we have established ourselves as a premier centre for comprehensive and affordable dental care.

We have had an exceptional degree of success across treatments as a result of our careful diagnosis, detailed treatment planning and using well established evidence based methods, techniques, instruments and materials.

Our friendly, compassionate and competent staff are dedicated to you and will ensure your comfort and quality care. We provide the best and latest in technology and treatment options. We provide gentle, family-oriented care to the adults and children of our community here and surrounding areas.

We look forward to welcoming you to our dental family. Our office is warm and inviting. You will always be greeted with a smile and treated with respect. You will play an active role in your treatment and your voice will be heard when you have questions or concerns.

At Confi-Dental Clinic & Implant centre our dentists are all Specialists in their particular fields of dentistry, be it Endodontisits, Implantologists, Pedodontists, Orthodontists, Prosthodontists, Periodontists, O.S. All our dentists are all highly trained and extremely qualified. We select only the best as we wish to deliver only the finest dental treatments to all our patients
